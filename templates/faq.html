Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your Himalayan adventure

Do I need a visa to visit Nepal?

Yes, most nationalities require a visa. You can obtain a visa on arrival at Tribhuvan International Airport in Kathmandu or apply in advance at a Nepalese embassy.

What is the best time to trek in Nepal?

The best seasons are Spring (March–May) and Autumn (September–November) when the weather is clear and views are spectacular.

Is trekking difficult? Do I need to be very fit?

We offer treks for all levels. Moderate fitness is recommended. Our guides will adjust the pace to suit your group.

Are your guides and staff insured?

Yes. All our guides, porters, and staff are fully insured and licensed by the Nepal Tourism Board.

What kind of accommodation is provided during the trek?

We use comfortable teahouses/lodges with basic rooms and shared bathrooms. Camping options are available for remote routes.

Do you provide porters?

Yes, we provide experienced porters who carry your main luggage so you can trek lightly with just a daypack.
