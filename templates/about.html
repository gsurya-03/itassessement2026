Our Story

Born in the shadow of the world's highest peaks, Himalaya Horizons was founded with one simple dream to share the magic of the Himalayas responsibly and passionately.

Who We Are

We are a locally owned adventure company based in Kathmandu, Nepal. With over a decade of experience, our team of expert Sherpa guides, knowledgeable local staff, and passionate travel enthusiasts curate unforgettable journeys into the heart of the Himalayas.

From the iconic Everest Base Camp to the serene Annapurna Sanctuary, from cultural tours in Kathmandu Valley to off-the-beaten-path treks, we believe every adventure should be safe, sustainable, and deeply meaningful.
